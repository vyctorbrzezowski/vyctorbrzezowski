I'm Vyctor, a Brazilian designer who ships code.

I graduated in Computer Engineering from UnB. Funny enough, I ended up caring less about the tech itself than about the experience of the people using it. And recently, agents.

My current mission is making LLM-generated UI and UX actually good. Not just steering models away from the usual slop patterns, but giving them better vocabulary, context, and product judgment so they can make stronger decisions: generating a UI from scratch, improving existing work, refactoring toward a design system, or reviewing PRs like a CI check.

I've worked around product and design for about 10 years, and this is the problem I keep coming back to: helping generated interfaces become more specific to the product, the domain, and the people using them every day.
