I'm Vyctor, a Design Engineer who builds tools for people and agents.

I build tools and automation systems for content, design, and coding workflows.

My current mission is making LLM-generated UI and UX actually good. Not just steering models away from the usual slop patterns, but giving them better vocabulary, context, design thinking, and product judgment so they can make stronger product decisions: generating a UI from scratch, improving existing work, refactoring toward a design system, or reviewing PRs like a CI check.

I’ve worked around product and design for about 10 years, and this is the problem I keep coming back to: helping generated interfaces become more specific to the product, the domain, and the people using them every day.
